THE CORE DISTINCTION
DraftSharks shows floor and ceiling from an internal model. We show floor, ceiling, boom probability, and bust probability — derived from the Vegas prop market. The difference: we tell you not just the range but the exact probability of landing at any point in it. No competitor does this.


The Problem
41 million people play fantasy football in the U.S. The serious ones pay for tools like FantasyPros, 4for4, and DraftSharks. Some of those tools show a floor and a ceiling. None of them can tell you the probability of reaching that ceiling. That requires a full probability distribution — and getting one requires reading the Vegas prop market, not estimating from historical data.

What NovaPredict Is
An AI-powered fantasy sports platform that reverse-engineers Vegas sportsbook prop probability curves into complete PPR distributions. For every player, every week: floor, median, ceiling, boom probability (chance of 20+ PPR points), and bust probability (chance of sub-10 PPR points). Built from the sharpest prediction market in sports, with six proprietary layers on top. Launching NFL, expanding to NBA, MLB, NHL, and beyond.

How We Arrive at Our Predictions
Five steps. No competitor does any of them.

	•	Pull every player prop from all active sportsbooks: primary lines, alt ladders (25+ through 200+ yards), TD ladders, combo props, line movement, and juice shifts.
	•	Strip the bookmaker’s margin using two-sided vig removal for primary lines and book-tier formulas for alt lines. Cross-book consensus with Pinnacle double-weighted.
	•	Fit a smooth probability curve through the alt ladder data using PCHIP interpolation. Extract floor, median, ceiling, boom%, and bust% directly from that curve.
	•	Run 100,000 Monte Carlo scenarios combining yards, receptions, and TD distributions — with correlations from combo props — to produce the final PPR distribution.
	•	Blend the market-derived distribution with our internal model (target share, snap share, game script, historical signals) proportional to a Market Confidence Score.

The Six Proprietary Layers — The Secret Sauce

Season Points Budget System
Every player’s season is a finite point pool. We track actual vs projected production weekly. A player 22% ahead of pace carries regression risk — and a lower boom probability — that the prop market resets every week and never sees.
DNA Volatility Grading
Every player graded S+ through X based on career consistency. DNA widens or narrows the distribution beyond what Vegas implies. An S+ bust% is genuinely low. An X bust% is genuinely dangerous.
Earned vs Gifted Classification
32 points on 10 catches is earned. 28 points on 4 catches and 3 TDs is gifted. Gifted scores regress at 3x the rate. Boom probability drops accordingly — even if the prop line hasn’t moved.
Role Change Detection
Snap/target share spikes 10+ points above the 4-week average? Role change. We identify it 48–72 hours before the market adjusts. Boom% and bust% shift immediately.
15-Year Historical Signal Library
WR2s on short weeks: −7.1% (n=1,840, STRONG). Post-2 TD regression: −41% TD rate (n=480, STRONG). Wind >20 mph on WR1: −16.4% (n=740, STRONG). Every signal adjusts the full distribution — not just the median.
Confidence Calibration System
Every projection carries a Market Confidence Score. We track accuracy by confidence tier and publish it. Users see what the model has actually achieved — not what the formula claims.
